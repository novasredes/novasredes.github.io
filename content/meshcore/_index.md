---
title: Meshcore
description: Low bandwidth chat app that uses LoRa
---

**Meshcore** exclusively uses the LoRa PHY which means it runs
on boards with a LoRa modem and generally operates around the
center frequencies of 434Mhz and 868Mhz.


This section is dedicated to the discussion of the [MeshCore project](https://meshcore.io/).
