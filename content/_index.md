Novasredes

O senhor is operating a community network for the use of
experimenting with new routing technologies.

The network does not provide access to what is considerdd
by the mainstream as "The Internet" (i.e. you can't use
YouTube on this network).

== Spectrum usage ==

## Bands

2.4Ghz and 5Ghz dual band WiFi equipment
LoRa equipment running in the 868 Mhz band

## Specifics

* Our LoRa usage is _on the dot_ 868Mhz (`868000` Khz)
* WiFi usage for 5GHz is on channel `X`
* WiFi usage for 2.4GHz is on channel `Y`

## Regras

* Nothing illegal
* No sharing of Internet access
