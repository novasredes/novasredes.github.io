## Things we need help with

### Hardware

The scaffolding hardware for mounting the equipment and the tools
as well are quite in need:

1. Tubular pipes
	* We mount brackets onto these
2. Antenna brackets
	* We mount these _to_ the tubes and then to the antennas
3. Antennas
	* Anything that is 2.4Ghz, 5Ghz or 868Mhz is what is in need
4. Electrical wire
	* Outdoor rated but please not some dense core cabling which
		is impossible to use in a screw terminal - it's overkill
		anyways.
5. Drill bits
	* Depending on where the mounting will be done, if you can
		lend a tool for a price or know somebody who can assist in
		the installation then that would be much appreciated

A lot of the technical equipment I either have or can get
access to - but some of the more basic materials - tubing
etc. does come at a price and assistance in purchasing those
would be helpful in the long run.

### Highsite access

Getting high-site access at an affordable price _or_ for free is
definately needed. Preferably on private property if possible,
whether commercial or houses - I do not mind - location and altitude
are most important.

This includes:

1. Tall buildings
	* These typically have large flat rooves already with existing wiring
		infrastructure that can be plugged into


If access is given to a site on local government property then
a price should be paid as I do **not** want this project to be
a burden on the tax payer in anyway - therefore it must have no
net impact on the funds of such properties (within whichever departement
they fall into).
